About US

It is a website which gives insight to the concept of futuristic education in the country. This website is made for the participation of Avgaahan 2023, a national level competition organised by Maitreyi College, University of Delhi. Futuristic education deals with the concept of advanced and inclusive learning media for the students, whether be it in the form of online classes or offline classes.

It also deals with the concept of online education, which is the future of education in the country. The world is a global village, and with the ascending of technology, the world is becoming more and more connected.

The concept of online education is the future of education in the country. It is a concept which is being adopted by many countries, and India is not far behind.

The website is made using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. The website is made using the concept of responsive web design, which makes the website compatible with all devices. The website is made using the concept of mobile first design, which makes the website compatible with all devices.
